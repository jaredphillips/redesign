ABOUT ME

Jared Phillips is web designer, developer, volunteer, golfer, photographer and painter based out of Toronto, Ontario.

Originally born in small town Alliston, Jared developed early on a passion for art and especially photography running the successful photoblog, crossbonesstyle for 10 years. This site showcased his usually usual photographys and artscraps.

After being a manager for close for 10 years, Jared decided to embrace his love of computers and enroll at BitmakerLabs to truly learn web development and not just dreamweaver. Upon graduation, Jared volunteered at numerous places including Ladies Learning Code and Kids Learning Code. He also worked full time as Student Advocate at Hacker You before becoming a freelancer.

Originally, self-taught in the early 1990's using just Photoshop & Dreamweaver, Jared has moved on to cutting edge technogies such as Angular, Rails, HTML5 and jQuery.
